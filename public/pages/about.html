Who We Are

KEMETION is a leading fantasy cricket platform dedicated to bringing the excitement of cricket to fans worldwide. We believe in providing a completely free, fair, and engaging platform where cricket enthusiasts can test their knowledge and skills.

Founded in 2023, KEMETION has quickly grown to become a trusted name in the fantasy sports industry. Our mission is to create a community where cricket fans can connect, compete, and celebrate the sport they love.
