Gameplay Questions

How many players should I select in my team? ‚ñº

You must select exactly 11 players for your team. This includes 1 captain, 1 vice-captain, and 9 other players. You can select players from both teams playing in the match.

What's the difference between Captain and Vice-Captain? ‚ñº

Your captain's points are multiplied by 1.5x, while your vice-captain's points are multiplied by 1.25x. Choosing the right captain and vice-captain is crucial for maximizing your team's score.

Can I change my team after the match starts? ‚ñº

No, your team is locked once the match starts. You cannot make any changes after the match begins. Make sure to finalize your team before the match starts.

How are points calculated? ‚ñº

Points are calculated based on real-time performance of your selected players in the match. Batsmen earn points for runs, bowlers for wickets, and fielders for catches. Check our "How to Play" page for the complete scoring system.

Can I create multiple teams for the same match? ‚ñº

Yes, you can create multiple teams for the same match and join different contests with each team. This allows you to experiment with different strategies.
