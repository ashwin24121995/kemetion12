Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a fantasy team?

Sign up, select a match, pick 11 players, set captain and vice-captain, and submit your team before the match starts.

How are fantasy points calculated?

Points are awarded based on player performance: runs, wickets, catches, and other cricket statistics.

When can I join contests?

You can join contests anytime before the match starts. Once the match begins, team creation is locked.

What are the age requirements?

KEMETION is available only for users aged 18 and above. Age verification is mandatory during registration.
