Rules & Regulations

  • Team Lock: Once a match starts, you cannot make changes to your team.
  • 11 Players Required: Your team must have exactly 11 players to be valid.
  • Captain & Vice-Captain: You must select one captain and one vice-captain from your team.
  • 100% Free: No real money is involved. This is purely a skill-based game.
  • Fair Play: Any attempt to manipulate scores or engage in unfair play will result in account suspension.
  • Age Requirement: You must be 18 years or older to participate.
